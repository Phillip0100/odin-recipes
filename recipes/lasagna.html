Lasagna

About this dish

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly!
This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Everyone loves a good lasagna, right? It's a great way to feed a crowd and a perfect dish to bring to a potluck.
It freezes well. It reheats well. Leftovers will keep you happy for days.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
