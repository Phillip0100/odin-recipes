Weeknight chili, here we come!
Ours is a chili recipe that you can make in just 40 minutes.

Everyone knows a good chili has a complex flavor, thanks in part
to a bunch of different spices at work. Cooks swear by all sorts of spices in their chili —
everything from cinnamon to mustard powder. But these are the four we'll pretty much always use:
chili powder, cumin, dried oregano, and paprika.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1/2 white onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 tbsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1 tsp. dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp. paprika
  • 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly fround black pepper
  • 1 (15-oz) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (28-oz) can crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot and add onion and cook until soft.
  2. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato paste and stir.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until no longer pink.
  5. Add chili powder, cumin, oregano, paprika, cayenne (if using).
  6. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  7. Pour in kidney beans and crushed tomatoes and bring to boil.
  8. Reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.
  9. Ladle into bowls and enjoy!
