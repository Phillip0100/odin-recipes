About

Making homemade pizza can sound like a lot of work, but it is so worth the bragging rights.
The pizza itself requires few ingredients and just a little bit of love and rest time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup of all purpose flour
  • 100 ml tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 onions
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 100 grams processed cheese
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1/2 capsicum(green pepper)
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella
  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • water as required

Steps

  1. Brush the dough top with olive oil.
  2. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with topping
  3. Slide pizza into the oven.
  4. Bake the pizza in the 475°F oven for about 10 to 15 minutes.
